About Our School

Provi Int'l is proposed to start in 2020 to help students develop their self confidence both personally and academically so they can achieve the very best results and take responsibility for their own success.

We work closely with each student to carve out a unique education programme that best suits their needs and ambitions, offering them the flexibility, choice and comprehensive support and guidance necessary to reach their goals. We are currently running an online distance learning programme for all of our students, delivering live streamed audio-visual lessons in which allows interaction between teachers and students.
